Spells

Get ready to create strong and powerful spells to aid you in the battlefield!

WHAT ARE SPELLS?

Once you reach Town Hall level 5, you are allowed to build the Spell Factory. Spells create auras on the battlefield, causing various effects, usually significantly increasing the overall offensive potential of the attacker.

At the highest level of play, spells are the deciding factor between “Victory” and “Defeat.” The Whole strategies are relying on spells.
